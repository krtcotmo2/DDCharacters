Pathfinder Character Management System

This application allows for easy storage and access to all aspects of a Pathfinder character. All characters are visible to all users but you can only edit characters that you control.

Once you log in the top navigation bar will have an option to load a character or view details for a loaded character. Character details include Stats, To hits, Armor Class, Saves, Skills, Feats, Notes, Equipment, and Memorized Spells.

DO NOT click on the refesh browser window as it will drop your token and you will need to log in again. To reload the page simple click on the tab you are currently viewing. The back and forward arrows of the browser work.
